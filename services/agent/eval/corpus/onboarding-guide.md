# Aurelia Corp Onboarding Guide

## Before day one

HR sends the welcome packet and equipment ships to your home address. New
hires receive a MacBook Pro, a 27-inch external monitor, a headset, and a
hardware security key. Sign the acceptable-use policy before your accounts
are activated.

## Your first week

Day one covers IT setup and a security briefing. Accounts are provisioned
for email, Slack, Jira, and the HR portal. Days two through five alternate
between team introductions, codebase walkthroughs, and your first starter
ticket. Friday closes with a retro with your manager.

## The buddy program

Every new hire is paired with an onboarding buddy — an experienced teammate
from a different squad who answers day-to-day questions, joins you for lunch
in week one, and checks in weekly for the first two months. Your buddy is
not your manager and conversations with them are informal.

## 30-60-90 plan

Your manager shares a 30-60-90 plan in week one: by day 30 you should ship
small changes independently; by day 60 you own a feature area; by day 90 you
participate in on-call rotation and quarterly planning.

## Probation

The probation period is three months with a mid-point check-in. Feedback
flows through weekly one-on-ones, so nothing at the check-in should be a
surprise.
