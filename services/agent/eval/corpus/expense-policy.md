# Aurelia Corp Travel & Expense Policy

## Booking travel

Book flights and hotels through the corporate travel portal. Flights under
six hours are economy class; premium economy is allowed for longer flights.
Hotel rates are capped by city tier; the portal shows the cap during
booking.

## Meals and per diem

The daily meal per diem for business travel is 75 USD (or local
equivalent). Per diem covers all meals and incidentals; alcohol is not
reimbursable. Team dinners hosted by a manager use the entertainment budget
instead of per diem.

## Receipts and reimbursement

Receipts are required for every expense over 25 USD. Submit expense reports
within 30 days of the trip through the finance portal; reimbursement lands
with the next payroll run after approval. Reports older than 90 days are
rejected except with CFO approval.

## Corporate cards

Directors and frequent travelers may request a corporate card. Card
statements reconcile monthly in the finance portal; personal spend on the
corporate card must be flagged and repaid within one billing cycle.

## Ground transport

Use public transit or rideshare where practical. Rental cars require
manager pre-approval and must include the corporate insurance option.
