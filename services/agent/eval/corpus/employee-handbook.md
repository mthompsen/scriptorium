# Aurelia Corp Employee Handbook

## Working hours

Aurelia Corp operates on flexible working hours. Core collaboration hours run
from 10:00 to 15:00 local time; outside those hours employees arrange their
schedule with their team lead. Full-time positions are 40 hours per week.

## Remote work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote
arrangements require director approval and a home-office safety
self-assessment. Remote workers must be reachable during core hours.

## Paid time off

Employees receive 25 days of paid time off per year, accrued monthly. Up to
5 unused days may be carried over into the next calendar year; carryover
days expire on March 31. PTO requests go through the HR portal and need
manager approval at least two weeks in advance for absences longer than
three days.

## Parental leave

Aurelia Corp provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new
parents, regardless of gender. Leave may be taken in up to two blocks within
the first year. An additional 8 weeks unpaid extension is available on
request.

## Sick leave

Sick leave is uncapped and requires a doctor's note from the fourth
consecutive day. Extended illness beyond four weeks moves to the long-term
disability process described in the benefits guide.
